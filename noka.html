# A Message

Hi Tuah. It's been a while. I'm not even sure why I'm writing this. There's a slim chance you'll ever see it, but Aiko says it might help to get my thoughts out- even if no one else ever reads them. I don't really see the point, but I trust them.
You've been gone for a while now, and it's been hard. You left on such short notice... especially with all the progress we had made on the ship. We've had to start from scratch because of it. Still, I guess you weren't cruel enough to leave us with nothing. The blueprints you left behind are the only reason we can even try again.
Part of me wants to blame you. Another part of me keeps telling myself you must have had a reason. I just can't shake the feeling that... if anyone should have known you were leaving, it would’ve been me.
I guess what I really want to know is why. I wanted to understand what was going on behind those amber and blue eyes of yours... but you never let me see.
More than anything, I just want you to come back. Even if I know the chances of that are almost nothing.
